Cancelling of the subscription will not affect your account in any way, it will only ensure you won't be charged after 90 days.

No contracts, no hidden charges, no setup fees. There is no long-term commitment and you can cancel your subscription at any time.

What is a Query?

Query is one analysis of the chosen keyword. In Black Friday Deal accounts you have 10 queries per day. It means that you can analyze 10 different keywords every day.
